About The Project

This project is to help inform people about the types of dangers that are threatening our honey bees. If people work together to help create a healthy global environment, we could help bees thrive for many more years. Today, bees are being affected by several different threats, such as herbicides, climate change, varroa mites and many other factors. Over the years the amount of honey that is being produced has declined because of a colony collapse disorder as well. Not only can the colony collapse disorder affect the amount of honey we produce, but this could also be a threat to our overall food production. Honey bees are very important to our daily lives. So, what will you do to save the bees?
